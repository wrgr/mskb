This pathway is for learners whose starting question is **"what does MS look like in the clinic, and how is it managed?"** Work through it top to bottom. Each step links to a concept page with prerequisites, learning objectives, papers, and resources.

## Steps

1. **[What is Multiple Sclerosis?](/mskb/concepts/foundations/what-is-ms/)** — definition, classification, how MS differs from other demyelinating diseases.
2. **[MS epidemiology and natural history](/mskb/concepts/foundations/ms-epidemiology/)** — who gets MS, where, and how the natural history has shifted with DMTs.
3. **[MS phenotypes](/mskb/concepts/foundations/ms-phenotypes/)** — RRMS, SPMS, PPMS, CIS, RIS.
4. **[Relapses and remission](/mskb/concepts/clinical/relapse-and-remission/)** — defining a relapse, recovery patterns, pseudo-relapse.
5. **[McDonald criteria and MRI](/mskb/concepts/diagnosis/mcdonald-criteria/)** — the formal diagnostic standard and how MRI supports it.
6. **[CSF and blood biomarkers](/mskb/concepts/diagnosis/csf-biomarkers/)** — oligoclonal bands, NfL, GFAP.
7. **[Disease-modifying therapies — overview](/mskb/concepts/therapeutics/dmt-overview/)** — how the DMT landscape is organized.
8. Pick a drug class:
    - [Immunomodulatory therapies](/mskb/concepts/therapeutics/immunomodulatory-therapies/)
    - [Immunosuppressive therapies](/mskb/concepts/therapeutics/immunosuppressive-therapies/)
    - [B cell–targeted therapies](/mskb/concepts/therapeutics/b-cell-targeted-therapies/)
9. **[Treatment monitoring and switches](/mskb/concepts/therapeutics/treatment-monitoring/)** — when to stay, escalate, or de-escalate.
10. **[Disease course and prognosis](/mskb/concepts/clinical/disease-course/)** — how progression is measured and predicted.
