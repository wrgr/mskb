    <p>

      </article>
      <article class="journey-mini-card" role="listitem">
        <h3>2. Build your set</h3>
        <p>Click any node to open its detail panel on the right of the graph, then press <em>Add papers to list</em>. Selections appear in <em>Relevant Papers</em> below and are shared with the Explorer.</p>
      </article>
      <article class="journey-mini-card" role="listitem">
        <h3>3. Export and teach</h3>
        <p>Once your list has a few papers, generate a phased reading plan or export markdown, JSON, or BibTeX from the tools at the bottom of the page.</p>
      </article>
    </div>
  </section>
