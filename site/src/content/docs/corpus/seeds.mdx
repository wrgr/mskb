The corpus starts from **46 expansion sources**: 40 hand-curated T1 seed papers plus 6 review anchors. One-hop expansion (forward + backward citations) runs from all 46. Cross-seed quality scoring uses the 40 seeds, with a bridge rule (`seed>=1 + anchor>=1`) for effective Tier 2 coverage.

Recency flags: ⚓ foundational anchor (read for framing, not current guidance) · ✅ current · ⚠️ aging (content still relevant but consult newer literature on specific claims)
