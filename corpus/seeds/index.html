Seeds & Review Anchors

The corpus starts from 46 expansion sources: 40 hand-curated T1 seed papers plus 6 review anchors. One-hop expansion (forward + backward citations) runs from all 46. Cross-seed quality scoring uses the 40 seeds, with a bridge rule (seed>=1 + anchor>=1) for effective Tier 2 coverage.

Recency flags: ⚓ foundational anchor (read for framing, not current guidance) · ✅ current · ⚠️ aging (content still relevant but consult newer literature on specific claims)

Tier 4 expert signal source (v1.1)

Tier 4 documents are not listed on this page. The authoritative T4 source is data/t4_expert_signal.yaml, which contains 52 concept anchor signals across 8 clinical concept areas. These papers are nominated by editors as required anchors for MSKB educational concept pages — targeting thin-coverage topics structurally under-served by the T2/T3 algorithms. The pipeline reads this YAML directly at retrieval time.

Learning engineering experiment. MSKB is an open educational resource built to support student access to MS research. The corpus was assembled and annotated with AI assistance (bibliometric pipeline + Claude API distillation). Content reflects the peer-reviewed literature as of the corpus build date; verify critical clinical claims against primary sources and current guidelines before applying them.
